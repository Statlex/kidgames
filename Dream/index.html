How to Make Your Wishes Come True with the Power of Thought?

There are many techniques for wish fulfillment, this app will focus on one of the most powerful ones – the visualization and translation.

The first thing you should do is to articulate your wish. It should cause you pleasant feelings such as joy, happiness and pleasure. Positive emotions make your wishes stronger and bring closer the moment when you reach your dream.

Second you need to enter into a state of relaxation. Choose the time when you will not be disturbed, take a comfortable position, close your eyes and relax. Let your body become light and weightless as a feather, trying to keep your mind calm.

Once you reach a state of relaxation, think of the happiest episode in your life. “Tune in” to the wave of joy and positivity. This will fill your thoughts with the necessary energy.

Third imagine your dream as if it has already come true. You’ve got what you want. Feel the joy and gratitude, and the whole range of positive emotions. Stay in this beautiful state for some time, enjoy it, and then open your eyes.

Fourth write your wish in this app and send it to the universe. Wait until it is translated and all your strengths, energy and positive fluids will go to the universe. Think about your wish at this time.

Congratulations! Your dream is already on the way to implementation. Now let it go easily and freely. Your “order” has already been received by the universe, so let it find the way to implement it.

A few important points:

 - Your wish must not harm people or the world. If the power of thought will be used for destructive plans, nothing good will come of this.

 - After using this technique, you shouldn’t just sit and wait. Help the higher powers, act and get active in the real world.

 - If you have a big dream to make come true, you should do this exercise more than once. The more creative energy you will manage to give to your wish, the faster it will turn into reality.
